Project Rationale

Flaties wants to provide a system for users to have a new experience when it comes to looking for flat. Flaties wants their users to feel more comfortable when moving into a flat, as they would have an idea of what they are going to get themselves into. Users that are looking for new flatmates can also know what to expect through the use of Flaties.

Research and development activities

Development process includes both researching and developing activities that consists of non-software and software-based project.

Researching of non-based project is the important part during the development activities. Besides several documents as IT project management, we have done a couple of researches consisting of market analysis including documents and surveys, websites similar to Flaties in AUS and CA, database analysis including documents and use case diagram, researches on security, service and social networks using in Flaties; additionally, to improve Flaties marketing as well as promote it on searching tools especially Google, we have made some researches on search engine optimization (SEO) that includes full version of SEO knowledge, how to use SEO in HTML5, SEO checklist that is to make sure that we meet all the SEO expectations.

Teamwork

At the beginning of this paper, we came up with a team agreement. In this team agreement, it basically contains all of the rules that the team has agreed on. It covers how the team will be developing, communicating, and the responsibility of each team member. Adding on, the team makes sures that every group meetings or discussions are held in a friendly matter. Each team member has the right to voice out their opinions an d the team will listen to it. The team also makes sures that group meetings are held in a professional matter so that everyone can stay comfortable with each other. This agreement is signed by all the team members to show formalize the agreement.

When developing a task from Asana, we assign ourselves into the user stories that we are working on by adding our avatar underneath a task. This practice avoids confusion and lets a team member know if a task is available for developing or not. This is a good way of organizing and keeping our Asana Board nice and tidy.

We often do Pair Programming or Mob Programming when we get together as a team. We find this technique to be very useful as it allows us to learn from each other. In Pair or Mob Programming, the whole team communicates with each one another. We get to involve everyone in the team and get different inputs on how to solve a problem. This approach is a continous code review, therefore the team becomes more efficient as we get to code and reviews the code at the same time.

Communication

The team meets up once a week for a group discussion. The team finds it important to meet once a week because we are able to have face to face communication as a team. Through these face to face meetings, we are able to see the body language of everyone. This gives us live feedback from one another. We also found out that weekly meetings will help us build relationships, enhance trust, increase activity and increase effectiveness towards work that needs to be done.

Moreover, we also have been doing By-Daily Standup Meetings. This allows us the team to have a daily plan and keep everyone in the team updated. This method lets the team know if someone is stuck with a current task or not.

You can view our group meetings and it's details on our Trello board.

Furthermore, the team also has weekly meetings with the teamâ€™s supervisor, Jim Buchan. In the supervisor meetings, we update Jim on all of the work that has been done or needs to be done towards the project. Jim gives us feedbacks from our work and also gives us advice going forward. Supervisor meeting minutes can be viewed here.

Since the team cannot see each other a lot due to other commitments outside of the project, we have been using Facebook Messenger as our main communication tool. Facebook messenger has allowed us to keep everyone updated, ask questions about the project, set up meetings and just communicate with one another. Moreover, the team uses Slack as another tool to communicate with each other. However, we usually use Slack for when we are communicating with our client.

Relationship with Client

Since the client does not have a proper workplace for us to come and do our work in, we have been meeting them at AUT grounds. The team usually meets the client every 3 weeks. For the times when do not see them in person, the team has been mostly keeping in touch with them through text messages, emails and on our Slack channel. We are also communicating with them on our Asana board.

Our client meeting minutes can be viewed on our Trello board, under 'Client Meetings'.

Peer Programming

Working together to achieve quality assurance.

On a weekly basis we meet as a group and often worked in pairs to achieve tasks, after a stand up meeting or sprint review we would get onto work. Even if we had individual tasks we would work close by at least once a week. Working in pairs or as a group we would learn from each other and achieve more faster!

Peer Review

Once a task completed it needs to be reviewed.

The purpose of doing a peer review is to reduce the amount of petenital bugs, less rework, better team communication and cohesiveness. Although a peer reivew can take time the benefits outweigh the cost on time.

On Asana the task is moved from 'In Progress' to 'Peer Review' Column. Over on GitLab a merge request is created for that feature to be merged to the develop branch.

Each change in code is reviewed and anything out of the ordinary is commented on to find the justification of why it was done.

If there are no required changes the merge request is accepted and the feature branch is merged to the develop branch. Over on Asana the task is moved to from 'Peer Review' to 'Done'

Researches on market analysis

Researches on market analysis (survey documents and business plan)

By researching online as well as via surveys from 50 participants about their hobbies, their needs, and their difficulties whenever they find accommodations to live, we have a general view of current markets as well as deep understand on their demands. After researching and analyzing, we can see that most of them are flatting or sharing house with others; and their inspections for new places includes be close to transport or motorway, tidy homes, parking allowed, pet allowed, and some of them wish to live with people who have the same interests or jobs so that they can learn and compete together to improve themselves.

Collection of some websites that similar to Flaties in Australia and California

To make sure that Flaties site can compete in the markets of accommodations, as well as to support to the future explanation of Flaties, we investigate some websites similar to Flaties in Australia and California, US.
