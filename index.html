Project Rationale

Flaties wants to provide a system for users to have a new experience when it comes to looking for flat. Flaties wants their users to feel more comfortable when moving into a flat, as they would have an idea of what they are going to get themselves into. Users that are looking for new flatmates can also know what to expect through the use of Flaties.

Peer Programming

Working together to achieve quality assurance.

On a weekly basis we meet as a group and often worked in pairs to achieve tasks, after a stand up meeting or sprint review we would get onto work. Even if we had individual tasks we would work close by at least once a week. Working in pairs or as a group we would learn of each other and achieve more faster!

Peer Review

Once a task completed it needs to be reviewed.

The purpose of doing a peer review is to reduce the amount of petenital bugs, less rework, better team communication and cohesiveness. Although a peer reivew can take time the benefits outweigh the cost on time.

On Asana the task is moved from 'In Progress' to 'Peer Review' Column. Over on GitLab a merge request is created for that feature to be merged to the develop branch.

Each change in code is reviewed and anything out of the ordinary is commented on to find the justification of why it was done.

If there are no required changes the merge request is accepted and the feature branch is merged to the develop branch. Over on Asana the task is moved to from 'Peer Review' to 'Done'
